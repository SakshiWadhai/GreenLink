ABOUT US

At GreenLink, we are committed to transforming waste management by bridging the gap between waste generators, recyclers, and farmers. Improper waste disposal leads to pollution, resource wastage, and environmental damage. Our platform provides a smart and efficient solution by connecting businesses and households with recyclers and waste processors, ensuring proper collection, tracking, and recycling of waste. As an additional feature, we also support organic waste management by linking organic waste generators with farmers. This allows farmers to access compostable waste from composting centers, promoting sustainable agriculture and reducing reliance on chemical fertilizers. With features like dual registration, waste tracking, transportation services, and real-time notifications, GreenLink simplifies the waste disposal process and encourages a circular economy where resources are reused and repurposed. Join us in building a cleaner, greener, and more sustainable future.
